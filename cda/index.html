Meet the Founder

Christine Juma is a passionate Software Engineer and Community Advocate based in Nairobi, Kenya. She believes everyone deserves a professional digital presence, regardless of their resources. Over the years, Christine has built websites for startups, educational initiatives, wellness practitioners, and community projects, helping people reach wider audiences and make an impact online.

Her mission is simple: empower individuals and organizations to grow, connect, and share their work through accessible, high-quality websites. By combining technical expertise with a heart for community, Christine turns ideas into digital reality.
